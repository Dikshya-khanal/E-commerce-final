What is E-Commerce?

E-commerce refers to the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet.
It encompasses a wide range of online business activities, including retail shopping, auctions, payment gateways, internet banking,
and ticket booking. E-commerce has revolutionized the way businesses operate, offering convenience, a wider reach, and cost efficiency,
while providing consumers with access to a vast array of products and services at competitive prices. E-commerce removes traditional geographical barriers.
